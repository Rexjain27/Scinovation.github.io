Our goal is to foster a creative environment where individuals can share their ideas and bring them to life through collaboration and teamwork. The event aims to ignite the young minds with the sense of using science for modern age innovations. We at AUR aims to promote science and its inter-discplinary roles. Further, we have keynote speakers, interactive workshops, and opportunities for attendees to showcase their ideas and receive feedback from science experts.

A panel discussion welcoming established professors of India to discuss the realities of the science innovation and their own experiences.

About:-

Science natak is a event where you can exhibit your acting skills. A.U.R. gives you the platform to perform an innovative drama on science

1

Rules

a) No. of participants should be 5 to 7.

b) Time limit should be 8 to 10 minutes.

c) Theme should be related to science.

d) Language - Hindi and English only.

e) Exceeding the time limit will lead to disqualification.

f) Use of any abusive/obscene language is prohibited.

g) Teams have to bring their own props.

h) Use of any inflammable/ hazardous elements are strictly prohibited.

i) Teams are requested to confirm the validity of props with the management team before bringing them.

j) Performance should be confined within the area, as defined by the organizers.

k) No professional assistance is permitted, all team members should be certified students of the institute. Please bring along your identity cards.
